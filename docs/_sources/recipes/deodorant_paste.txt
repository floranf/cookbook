Deodorant Paste
===============

Ingredients:
------------
    * [30 g] **coconut oil** 
    * [20 g] **shea butter** 
    * [10 g] **beeswax** 
    * [15 g] **corn starch** 
    * [10 g] **baking soda** 
    * [5 drops] **lavender** 

Steps:
------
    #.  In a double boiler melt A to E. 
    #.  When melted add F mix well.
    #.  Pour in a small glass container. 
    #.  Let cool overnight.
