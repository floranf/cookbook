Banana Bread
============

Ingredients:
------------
    * [1-1/2 cup] **flour (all purpose, unbleached)** 
    * [1/2 cup] **wheat germ** 
    * [1/4 cup] **flax seeds**  -- ground
    * [1/4 cup] **brown sugar** 
    * [1 tsp] **salt** 
    * [1/2 tbsp] **baking powder** 
    * [1/2 tbsp] **baking soda** 
    * [3] **spotted bananas**  -- mashed
    * [1 tbsp] **leamon juice** 
    * [1/4 cup] **oil** 
    * [3 tbsp] **chia seeds**  -- soked, optional
    * [3/4 cup] **dates, chopped** 
    * [3/4 cup] **wallnuts, chopped** 
    * [3/4 cup] **chocolate**  -- optional
