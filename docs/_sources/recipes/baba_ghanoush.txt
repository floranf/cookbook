Baba ghanoush
=============

Ingredients:
------------
    * [1] **eggplant** 
    * [1 clove] **garlic** 
    * [1/4 cup] **thaini** 
    * [to taste] **salt** 

Steps:
------
    #. Slice the eggplant in haft lenghtwise. 
    #. Spread some olive oil on the eggplant surface. 
    #. Broil the eggplant, turning it from time to time. 
    #. Once the flesh of the eggplant is soft set aside to cooldown. 
    #. Once cold, mix the eggplant with all other ingredient in the food processor.
