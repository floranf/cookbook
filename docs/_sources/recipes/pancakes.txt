Pancakes
========

Ingredients:
------------
    * [1 cup] **flour** 
    * [1/2 tsp] **salt** 
    * [2 tbsp] **baking powder** 
    * [2 tbsp] **sugar** 
    * [1] **egg** 
    * [1 cup] **milk** 
    * [2 tbsp] **oil** 

Steps:
------
    #.  Mix all ingredient in a bowl. 
    #.  Bake a low heat in a cast iron pan covered with a lid.
