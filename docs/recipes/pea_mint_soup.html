Pea and Mint Soup

Ingredients:

  • [2] carrots – pealed and chopped
  • [2] celery – chopped
  • [2] onios – diced
  • [2 cloves] garlic
  • [some] olive oil
  • [1-1/4 litre] broth
  • [5-1/2 cups] frozen green peas
  • [hand full] fresh mint
  • [some] salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. <A-E> Add to a big pot.
  2. (10 mins) Cook till onions are golden.
  3. <F,G> Add to vegies
  4. (10 min) Simmer
  5. <H> Add to vegies
  6. [immersion blender] Pulse till smoth.
