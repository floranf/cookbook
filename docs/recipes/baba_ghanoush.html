Baba ghanoush¶

Ingredients:¶

  • [1] eggplant
  • [1 clove] garlic
  • [1/4 cup] thaini
  • [to taste] salt

Steps:¶

  1. Slice the eggplant in haft lenghtwise.
  2. Spread some olive oil on the eggplant surface.
  3. Broil the eggplant, turning it from time to time.
  4. Once the flesh of the eggplant is soft set aside to cooldown.
  5. Once cold, mix the eggplant with all other ingredient in the food processor.
