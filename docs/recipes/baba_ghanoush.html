Baba Ghanoush

Ingredients:

  • [1] eggplant
  • [1 clove] garlic
  • [1/4 cup] tahini
  • [to taste] salt

Steps:

  1. Slice the eggplant in haft lengthwise.
  2. Spread some olive oil on the eggplant surface.
  3. Broil the eggplant, turning it from time to time.
  4. Once the flesh of the eggplant is soft set aside to cooldown.
  5. Once cold, mix the eggplant with all other ingredients in the food processor.
