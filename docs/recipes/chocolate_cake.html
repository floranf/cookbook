Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • [241g, 1-1/2 cups] unbleached white flour
  • [31g, 1/3 cup] unsweetened cocoa powder
  • [7g, 1 teaspoon] baking soda
  • [4g, 1/2 teaspoon] salt
  • [111g] sugar
  • [100g, 1/2 cup] vegetable oil
  • [211g, 1 cup] cold water
  • [7g, 2 teaspoons] vanilla extract
  • [33g, 2 tablespoons] cider vinegar

Steps:

  1. (325 F) Preheat the oven.
  2. Sift together the flour, cocoa, soda, salt, and sugar.
  3. Mix together the oil, cold water and vanilla.
  4. Mix the liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients till batter is smooth.
  5. Mix in the vinegar and stir quickly.
  6. (18 minutes) Bake.
