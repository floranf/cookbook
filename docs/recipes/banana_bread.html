Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • [1-1/2 cup] flour (all purpose, unbleached)
  • [1/2 cup] wheat germ
  • [1/4 cup] flax seeds – ground
  • [1/4 cup] brown sugar
  • [1 tsp] salt
  • [1/2 tbsp] baking powder
  • [1/2 tbsp] baking soda
  • [3] spotted bananas – mashed
  • [1 tbsp] leamon juice
  • [1/4 cup] oil
  • [3 tbsp] chia seeds – soked, optional
  • [3/4 cup] dates, chopped
  • [3/4 cup] wallnuts, chopped
  • [3/4 cup] chocolate – optional

Steps:

  1. (375℉) Heat the oven.
  2. In a plate mash the banana.
  3. Add lemon juice, oil, brown sugar and chia seed.
  4. I a large bowl mix, flour, wheat germ, flax, salt, baking powder and baking soda.
  5. Add the bananas mix to the flour mix and mix well.
  6. Fold in the date, nuts and chocolate
  7. Pour the mix in a loaf pan lined with parchemin paper.
  8. Decorate with rolled oats.
  9. Bake for 45min, then
