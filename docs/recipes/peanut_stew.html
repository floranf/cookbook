Peanut Stew

Ingredients:

  • [5 ml] olive oil
  • [1 medium] sweet onion – diced
  • [3 cloves] garlic – minced
  • [1] red bell pepper – diced
  • [1] jalapeño – seeded and diced (optional)
  • [1 medium] sweet potato – peeled and chopped into 1cm cubes
  • [1 28-ounce/793-g can] diced tomatoes – with their juices
  • [some] sea salt
  • [some] freshly ground black pepper
  • [1/3 cup, 5 tbsp ,75 mL] peanut butter
  • [4 cups, 1L] vegetable broth
  • [1/2 tsp] chili powder
  • [stick] cayenne pepper – (optional)
  • [15-ounce/425-g can] chickpeas – drained and rinsed
  • [2 handfuls] baby spinach or kale leaves – destemmed

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. (5 min) , Sauté the onion and garlic, until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the bell pepper, jalapeño, sweet potato, and tomatoes with their juices.
  4. Raise the heat to medium-high and simmer for 5 minutes more.
  5. Whisk together the peanut butter with some vegetable broth.
  6. Stir the mixture into the vegetables along with the rest of the broth, chili powder, and the cayenne.
  7. Cover the pan with a lid and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  8. (10-20 min) , Cover and simmer until the sweet potato is fork-tender.
  9. Stir in the chickpeas and spinach.
  10. Cook until the spinach is wilted.
  11. Season with salt and black pepper.
  12. Serve with a garnish of cilantro and roasted peanuts.
