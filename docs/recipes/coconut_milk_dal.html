Coconut Milk Dal¶

Ingredients:¶

  • [1] Medium onion – diced
  • [1 clove] garlic – minced
  • [2 cups] kale – destemed and choped
  • [1 cup] red lentils – well rinsed
  • [1 tbsp] curry paste
  • [some] salt
  • [some] pepper
  • [400ml] coconut milk
  • [400ml] water

Steps:¶

  1. Cook the onion and garlic till golded.
  2. Work the cury paste in.
  3. Add the lentils and mix well.
  4. Add the kale and cook till it start to wilt.
  5. Add the coconut water.
  6. Simmer.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
